About me

From childhood in Uruguay to adult life in Italy, my journey has been a whirlwind of changes and adaptations. I was born in Uruguay, where every corner resonated with the music of tango and the aroma of mate. At 16 years old, the direction of my life changed drastically when my family moved to the United States... It was an immense culture shock: the Spanish of my childhood mixed with the English of my new environment. Despite linguistic and cultural challenges, I found my place in the land of opportunity, and successfully completed my high school. After 7 years in the United States, my trip took me to Italy. Amid the picturesque streets and tantalizing aromas of Italian cuisine, I immersed myself in the world of web development. Here, each line of code became a bridge between my past and my future, between my Uruguayan roots and my Italian present. My story is woven with threads of resilience and determination. Every linguistic obstacle and cultural challenge became opportunities to grow and learn. Now, as a Full-Stack developer, I combine my love for technology with my ability to adapt to new environments. I am a reflection of my past, a traveler between worlds, and I am ready to face any challenge that the future has in store for me.
